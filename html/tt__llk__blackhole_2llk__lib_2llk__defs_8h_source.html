116 /*
117 Stochastic rounding modes:
118  None: No stochastic rounding enabled, default rounding is round to nearest even.
119  Fpu: Enables stochastic rounding for every accumulation in the fpu
120  Pack: Enables stochastic rounding in both gasket and packer. Gasket rounding is in
121  data format conversion stage from dest format to pack_src_format. Packer rounding
122  is in data format conversion stage from pack_src_format to pack_dst_format.
123  All: Enables fpu, pack and gasket rounding.
